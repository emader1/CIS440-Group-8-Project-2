Manager

Managers are senior professionals who oversee a team of Mentors and Mentees. They are responsible for setting goals, assigning tasks, and providing guidance to both Mentors and Mentees.


Mentor

Mentors are experienced individuals who provides guidance, support, and knowledge to Mentees. Mentors share their expertise, offer advice, and help Mentees develop their skills and achieve their career goals.


Mentee

Mentees are less experienced professionals who seeks guidance and mentorship from more experienced individuals, such as Mentors. Mentees are eager to learn, grow, and advance their careers with the support and advice of their Mentor.
